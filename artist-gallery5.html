Emer Quinn

Bio

Emer Quinn is a recent graduate of the National College of Art and Design. She majored in photography, photo editing and digital art. Her work focuses on singular images and she uses contrasting light to capture the beauty of an given object's form. Emer is inspired by Andy Warhol's work in which the famous artist used prints of seemingly mundane objects to make political statements about contemporary culture. Emer is more interested in bringing out the beauty and aesthetics of these objects.
