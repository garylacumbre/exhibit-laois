Brian Mulcahy

Bio

Brian is an associate member of the IPPVA (Irish Professional Photographers and Videographers Association), and has been creating photographic art since 2010. He is renowned locally for his relaxed style in reportage weddings and the quality of the images he produces. His other passion is landscape photography and portraits where he excels in capturing the essence of both people and place.From his portrait studio in Abbeyleix, he works closely with clients, creating beautiful, award-winning portrait images.
