Gillian Flynn

Bio

Gillian is a native of Laois, hailing from the rolling hills of Stradbally. She has been painting landscapes around Laois for as long she she had lived here. In her latest work, Gillian gets bitten by the travelling bug and unveils her passion for landscapes by bringing us work completed on her travels to North and South America.
