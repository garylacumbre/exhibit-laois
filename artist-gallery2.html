Hope Abaya

Bio

Hope came to Ireland in 2015 from her home country of Sudan. Despite her harsh upbringing and the dangers of violent extremism, she decided to live as her name suggests and surround herself with colour. Hope won a scholarship to study at National College of Art and Design and was inspired to bring the colourful fashion designs of her home country into the new medium of textile art. Her work is intricate, complex but yet full of soul and enjoyment.
