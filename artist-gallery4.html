Fion Molloy

Bio

Fion has been painting the people of Laois for the past ten years, constantly inspired by the changing nature of the people living here and the growing diversity that the County offers. In his latest work, Fion has taken inspiration from this diversity and has decided to celebrate Laois' people by injecting an elementing of fun and surprise to his latest portraits.
